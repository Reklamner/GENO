    Your Digital

    Familiar

    A Genopet is your digital spirit animal encoded with your personality that evolves through your daily activity. Your Genopet will join you as you traverse the Genoverse. Stay active to guide the destiny of your Genopet and unlock your true potential. As you evolve⁠—it evolves.

    A True

    Role-Playing Game

    Genopets is the first Move-to-Earn mobile RPG that rewards you for exercising your body, mind, and spirit through the care of your digital familiar. Transform your physical movement and cognitive exertion into in-game progression to upgrade and evolve your Genopet NFT. As you customize your Genopet in your likeness, you’ll find that it’s as rare and valuable as you are.

    Challenge Your Friends

    Enter the Battle Arena to battle other players as you sharpen your reaction time, memory, and overall cognitive skills through turn-based mini games. Play-for-Keeps to reap wagered items and move-sets from your defeated opponents. Will you be the next Arena Champion?

    Play & Earn

    Sell your hard-earned Genopets NFTs in the open marketplace while you take steps to earn $KI from your Habitat. Use $KI to either craft special items to upgrade your Genopet’s aesthetics and skills or sell your $KI to other players in need of a boost. Summon your Genopet and start trading your sweat for crypto.
